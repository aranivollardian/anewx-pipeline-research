NZ market assessment for AnewX

NZ is not a near-term priority for AnewX. The market is dominated by local players (Fletcher, Fulton Hogan, Downer, HEB) with established NZ piling specialists (Brian Perry Civil, Spiral Drillers). The Harbour Crossing is too far out (2029+). NZ is worth monitoring for the PE thesis (sector scale) but not for AnewX's near-term whale hunt.

Methodology notes

  • Whale scores: Subjective assessment by Vollardian based on: (1) project piling content, (2) AnewX win rate with head contractor, (3) geographic feasibility, (4) timing certainty, (5) competitive intensity, (6) barriers to entry. Scores 1-10.
  • Infrastructure health ratings: Based on IPA Budget Monitor rankings, 4-year capital program trends, federal allocation shares, and workforce demand projections from Infrastructure Australia Market Capacity Report.
  • Revenue estimates for competitors: Based on IBISWorld profiles, Piling Federation tier classifications, and publicly available data. Marked as estimates.
  • "Piling relevance": Assessed based on project type. Bridges, tunnels, rail stations, marine structures, stadiums, slope stabilisation = HIGH. Road upgrades, building fit-out = MODERATE/LOW.
